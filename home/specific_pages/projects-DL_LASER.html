Deep learning for LASER time series forecasting In May 2026 me and my project group for the Master's course Deep Learning applied deep learning solutions on a LASER measurements dataset. We found a hidden pattern in the data that lead to some interesting results. The article can be downloaded here.

Abstract

Time-series forecasting is a common problem. This paper attempts to apply RNNs, LSTMs, and transformers to predict a laser measurement signal. A custom heuristic was used to tune the hyperparameters. The best performing RNN achieved an MAE of 35.08 and an MSE of 2129, which was a significant improvement compared to the baseline with an infinite error.
